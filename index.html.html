What is Hydrogen Water?

Hydrogen water is simply water infused with molecular hydrogen (H₂) gas. Unlike regular water (H₂O), which already contains hydrogen atoms bonded with oxygen, hydrogen water has additional dissolved hydrogen molecules that act as powerful antioxidants.
